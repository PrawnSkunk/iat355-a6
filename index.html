About

Of the world’s 7 billion people, 4.3 billion remain without access to the internet. In 2013, Facebook launched Free Basics, a global partnership with the goal of making internet access available, without any financial cost to the user.

We want to track Facebook’s rollout of free mobile broadband internet across Africa, and other developing nations. Our objective is to develop a timeline of where Free Basics is either online, prohibited, terminated, or planned for future rollout. This is significant, because the information is not publically available anywhere, and such a dataset does not exist yet.
